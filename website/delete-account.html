Request Account Deletion

Option 1: Delete via App (Recommended)

You can instantly delete your account and all associated data directly within the Agricola app:

  1. Open the Agricola app on your device.
  2. Go to your Profile tab.
  3. Tap on Settings (gear icon).
  4. Scroll down and select Delete Account.
  5. Confirm your choice. This action is irreversible.

Option 2: Request via Email

If you cannot access the app, you may request account deletion via email. We will process your request within 7 business days.

Please email us at:

dumaleng@nalediapps.com

Subject: Agricola Account Deletion Request
Body: Please include the email address associated with your account.

Data Deletion Details

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and profile photos.
  • Farm Data: All registered crops, harvest records, and farm details.
  • Marketplace Listings: Any active products listed for sale.
  • App Preferences: Language settings and notifications.

Data Retention

We may retain certain data for specific periods as required by law or for legitimate business purposes:

  • Transaction History: Records of completed sales/orders are kept for financial reporting and fraud prevention (retained for up to 30 days).
  • Anonymized Analytics: Aggregated usage data that cannot be linked back to you personally.
